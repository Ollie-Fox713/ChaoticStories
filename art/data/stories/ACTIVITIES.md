# Story Activity Chains

Quick-reference index of all job/activity chains across the four tribes.
Each chain groups related daily stories by activity type for future quest-building.

## Danian Activities

### Carnival Trips (`chain_dan_carnival`)
- **Type:** Recreation
- **Description:** Recreational visits to the Carnival of Confusion -- gravity mazes, sensory swaps, and sanctioned chaos.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_17.json
  - "Carnival Visit"
- **Locations:** Carnival of Confusion, Mount Pillar

### Hive Construction (`chain_dan_construction`)
- **Type:** Construction
- **Description:** Digging new tunnels, repairing ceilings, producing and applying mucus sealant. The hive never stops expanding.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_02.json, daily_dan_07.json, daily_dan_18.json
  - "The Tunnel Crew"; "Ceiling Repair"; "Mucus Production"
- **Locations:** Iron Pillar, Mount Pillar, Queen's Gate

### Embassy Duty (`chain_dan_embassy`)
- **Type:** Diplomacy
- **Description:** Cleaning and maintaining the OverWorld Embassy inside Mount Pillar -- fighting mold, preserving documents, and tolerating wooden furniture.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_14.json
  - "Embassy Cleaning"
- **Locations:** Mount Pillar, Overworld Embassy at Mount Pillar

### Structural Engineering (`chain_dan_engineering`)
- **Type:** Engineering
- **Description:** Quarterly stress surveys of the great pillars, crack measurement, and monitoring structural integrity after tremors.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_12.json, daily_dan_20.json, daily_dan_24.json
  - "Structural Survey"; "Root Trimming"; "The Tremor Report"
- **Locations:** Iron Pillar, Mount Pillar, Stone Pillar, Wooden Pillar

### Fungus Farming (`chain_dan_farming`)
- **Type:** Agriculture
- **Description:** Growing, tending, and harvesting bioluminescent fungus -- Mount Pillar's primary food source. Includes composting substrate and spreading spores to new garden chambers.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_01.json, daily_dan_13.json, daily_dan_25.json
  - "Fungus Farming"; "The Compost Churn"; "Spore Spreading"
- **Locations:** Gloomuck Swamp, Mount Pillar, Mount Pillar Reservoir

### Foraging & Nutrients (`chain_dan_foraging`)
- **Type:** Gathering
- **Description:** Hauling nutrient-rich mud from Gloomuck Swamp and gathering edible roots and insects from the swamp edge.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_06.json, daily_dan_11.json
  - "The Nutrient Run"; "The Foraging Party"
- **Locations:** Gloomuck Swamp, Overworld Embassy at Mount Pillar

### Hive Defense (`chain_dan_guard`)
- **Type:** Military
- **Description:** Gate security, lookout shifts, night patrols, and emergency evacuation drills. Defending the hive from all threats.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_05.json, daily_dan_09.json, daily_dan_15.json, daily_dan_22.json
  - "Guard Rotation at the Gate"; "The Lookout Shift"; "Swarm Drill"; "Night Patrol"
- **Locations:** Mount Pillar, Oipont's Lookout, Queen's Gate, Sands of the Unseen

### Hive Song (`chain_dan_hivesong`)
- **Type:** Culture
- **Description:** Practicing the communal Mugic that structurally reinforces Mount Pillar's foundations. Every voice matters.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_04.json
  - "Hive Song Practice"
- **Locations:** Grand Hall of Muge's Summit, Mount Pillar

### Nursery Duty (`chain_dan_nursery`)
- **Type:** Caretaking
- **Description:** Tending larval pods in the nursery chambers -- temperature control, transport, and developmental monitoring.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_08.json
  - "Larva Tending"
- **Locations:** Mount Pillar, Mount Pillar Reservoir

### Message Relay (`chain_dan_relay`)
- **Type:** Communications
- **Description:** Operating the verbal message relay chain that carries orders across the entire hive in under four minutes.
- **Stories:** daily_dan_19.json
  - "The Message Relay"

### Chitin Recycling (`chain_dan_salvage`)
- **Type:** Recycling

- **Stories:** daily_dan_23.json

### Muge Politics (`chain_dan_summit`)

### Trail Maintenance (`chain_dan_trails`)

### Water Management (`chain_dan_water`)
